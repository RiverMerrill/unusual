Welcome to Unusual!

Unusual is a group of musicians, visual artist, and other creatives located in Boise, ID.
The goal of Unusual is to form a community of artists that can collaborate to make beautiful things.
If you'd like to join our little community, feel free to contact us at unusualrecordsboise@gmail.com.

Meet the Group

This is our group so far. Close friends and collaborative artistic partners.

River Merrill

River is the leader of Unusual. He is a musician and producer and created the album "Unusual", which sparked this collective.

Graham Olson

Graham is the sole member of the musical project "Floral Arrangment" and another founding member of Unusual. His debut album is set to release this year.

Janae Nilo

Janae is 1/2 of the project "Cactus Coy" (along with River) and photographer/designer for Unusual.
